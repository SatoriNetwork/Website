Purpose of the Grant: The purpose of this grant is to support research in the areas of Timeseries Forecasting and GPT Research.
2. SCOPE OF RESEARCH
Research Description: The Researcher shall conduct research focused on distributed prediction using a federation of machine learning (ML) models and examining the effects of the latent pool.
Objectives and Expected Outcomes:
Integration of the Chronos model and similar models to enhance the accuracy of timeseries forecasting within the Satori Neuron.
Efficient use of CPU, GPU, and multi-GPU systems.
Development of additional timeseries predicting GPT models.
Exploration and development of online learning within the GPT context.
Advancement of the "latent pool" concept.
3. GRANT AMOUNT AND PAYMENT TERMS
Total Grant Amount: The total grant amount is $7,500.
Payment Schedule: The Grantor shall disburse payments of $2,500 per month for a duration of three (3) months, beginning on the Effective Date.
Duration: This Agreement shall commence on May 25, 2024, and conclude on August 24, 2024.

The Researcher agrees to participate in bi-weekly meetings to discuss progress and challenges.
